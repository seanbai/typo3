The local authority of Limburg, a region in the Netherlands, wants to solve structural problems through a program called Social Agenda 2025. In collaboration with Talent in Bedrijf and CEVA Logistics the problems on the labour market are tackled. 

Talent in Bedrijf is a project together with the business world, whereby education is developed for people who can not easily find a spot on the labour market. Besides that, it is not only focused on theory about the business, but there is also focus on extra support like learning the Dutch language.

Ceva Logistics is as a supply chain management company always looking for logistic employees. Therefore, CEVA started an initiative to assist interns with their first step in the world of logistics. The interns work on different parts of the logistic proces, which ensures that they get to know the entire proces eventually. The aim of Talent in Bedrijf goes well with the initiative of CEVA.
