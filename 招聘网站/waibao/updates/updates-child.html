The future of supply chain

02 November 2017

The annual meeting of Chief Supply Chain Officer Forum – Europe took place from 16 till 18 October in Venlo, the Netherlands. This congress is part of the 3PL Summit event. At this congress, the executives of the leading supply chain and logistics companies come together to exchange information and discuss the key themes. This years topics focused on the future of supply chain: customer-centric and technology-driven.

Our managing director of the Benelux Bart Beeks attended this congress and also spoke about CEVA Logistics. In his speech, the emphasis was on the importance of people, because they make the difference. Even though the world becomes more technical and digital, the supply chain and logistics sector is and stays a people business.
