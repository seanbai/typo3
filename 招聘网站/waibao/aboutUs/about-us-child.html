What we do

Our services are divided into Contract Logistics, Transport Management, and Freight Management.

Contract Logistics

We perform all kinds of logistics services for our customers within Contract Logistics: transport, storage, value added logistics, and also worldwide third-party logistics or complete supply chain activities. We have built a strong reputation in this area and have long-standing relationships with a diversity of customers. With the combined knowledge and experience of our operations managers and supply chain designers, we design, implement, and manage logistics solutions and thereby create added value to our customers’ businesses.

Transport Management

Our expertise in transport management & planning enables us to find a solution to every customer need. Through our global network of control towers and state-of-the-art technology, we are able to monitor and respond effectively to situations such as bad weather, extra pick-ups and drop-offs, and traffic jams. This way, we can guarantee that our customers’ goods are always transported via the most efficient route.

Freight Management

Air Freight

In the dynamic sector of air freight, it is important for companies to have a partner who can keep the stability and reliability of the supply chain under control. Whether for shipments via commercial airlines or cargo aircraft, CEVA offers customized solutions for all types of air freight. We are one of the biggest air freight transport providers in the world and we offer an extensive range of premium added-value services and specialized products.

Ocean Freight

CEVA is also one of the world’s biggest shipping agents in the ocean freight sector. Thanks to our intensive and long-standing relationships with shipping lines, we can provide customers access to a global portfolio of carriers, the largest ports, the best transit times, and flexible planning. We also offer a wide range of Ocean freight services for both Less-than-Container Load (LCL) and Full-Container Load (FCL) shipments. This is supported with extensive Ocean Freight Management Services such as Break-bulk, Project Forwarding, and Partial & Full Charter Services.
